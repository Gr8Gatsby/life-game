Privacy Policy

Overview

Life Game is a Conway's Game of Life simulator designed for creativity and exploration. We value your privacy and built the app to operate with minimal data collection.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information, analytics, or device identifiers. All simulation data you create stays on your device.

Optional Diagnostics

If enabled in Settings, the app can send crash reports and performance logs using Apple's system services. These reports remove personally identifiable information and are used solely to improve stability.

Third-Party Services

Life Game does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social sharing SDKs.

Your Choices

  • You can disable optional diagnostics at any time from the in-app Settings panel.
  • You may delete the app to remove all stored data. User configurations and patterns are kept locally and will be erased during deletion.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Significant changes will be announced in the app release notes and on this page, with the effective date updated above.
