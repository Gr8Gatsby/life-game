Life Game

A serene playground for exploring Conway’s Game of Life. Paint classic patterns, watch emergent structures bloom, and learn how simple rules give rise to complex behavior.

Marketing Description

Life Game distills the legendary cellular automaton into an immersive, touch-friendly experience. Stamp famous Life formations, customize colors, and control simulation speed with intuitive gestures. Auto-zoom keeps every glider and pulsar in view, while analytics highlight population trends across generations. Whether you are teaching computational thinking or simply unwinding, Life Game reveals the beauty hidden inside Conway’s four simple rules.
